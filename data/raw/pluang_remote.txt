Pluang Remote Work Policy (2025 Edition)

1. Eligibility
All engineering staff are eligible for "Hybrid-First" working models. 
Technicians and security staff must be on-site.

2. The 3-2 Rule
Employees must be visible in the Jakarta or Bangalore office 3 days a week.
The remaining 2 days are flexible remote days. 
Wednesday is a mandatory "All-Hands" day in the office.

3. Equipment
Pluang provides a one-time stipend of $500 for home office setup.
MacBook Pros are standard issue for dev roles.
